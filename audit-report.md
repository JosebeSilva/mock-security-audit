**Recommendations**

Based on the audit findings, Botium Toys should prioritize implementing stronger access control mechanisms, including least privilege and separation of duties, to reduce the risk of unauthorized access to sensitive information. The organization should also deploy encryption technologies to protect customer payment data and personally identifiable information (PII/SPII) both at rest and in transit.

Additionally, Botium Toys should establish a formal disaster recovery plan and implement regular backups to improve business continuity and resilience against cyber incidents such as ransomware attacks or system failures. Deploying an intrusion detection system (IDS) would also improve the company’s ability to detect suspicious or malicious activity within the network.

Finally, the company should strengthen its password policy by enforcing modern password complexity requirements and implementing a centralized password management system. Regular asset inventory management and scheduled monitoring of legacy systems should also be established to improve compliance, reduce operational risk, and strengthen the organization’s overall cybersecurity posture.
